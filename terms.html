Services Provided

INVERSSYS provides managed cybersecurity services including, but not limited to:

  • Security Operations Center (SOC) monitoring — 24/7 or business-hours depending on the selected plan
  • Managed Detection and Response (MDR) — threat detection, behavioral analytics, and automated containment
  • Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) — log aggregation, correlation, and alerting
